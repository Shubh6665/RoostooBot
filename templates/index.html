Reinforcement Learning

The bot uses Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO), a state-of-the-art reinforcement learning algorithm that learns optimal trading strategies through direct market interaction.

Real-time Trading

Connect to the Roostoo mock exchange API to execute trades automatically. The bot analyzes market data and executes optimal trades based on its trained model.

Risk Management

Built-in risk management features ensure your assets are protected. Configure risk tolerance levels and the bot will adapt its trading strategy accordingly.

Getting Started
  1. Go to the Settings page to configure your trading preferences
  2. Select your preferred trading pair (e.g., BTC/USD)
  3. Set your risk level (percentage of portfolio to risk per trade)
  4. Start the bot and monitor its performance on the Dashboard
